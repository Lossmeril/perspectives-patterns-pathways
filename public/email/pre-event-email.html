Final Details & Schedule

Perspectives, Patterns, and Pathways Conference | 1st April 2025

Dear Attendees,

The Perspectives, Patterns, and Pathways (PPP) Conference is just around the corner, taking place on April 1st, 2025, from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We're excited to welcome you and want to share some final updates to help you prepare.

🕐 Schedule

Due to time constraints at our main venue, the day will begin in a different location:

  • 10:00 AM – Opening keynote by Dr. Michael Waugh in Ellison Building B 003, Northumbria University
  • 11:00 AM – We’ll move to Sandyford Building 405 for the remainder of the conference

Throughout the day, we’ll host a series of panels featuring artists, researchers, and cultural workers from a variety of disciplines. These sessions will explore different critical and creative approaches to infrastructures and systems — expect a rich and interdisciplinary set of conversations.

🎟 On-the-Spot Registration

If you or a colleague arrive without a ticket, on-the-spot registration will be available at the venue. We encourage pre-registration to expedite the check-in process, but we welcome walk-ins as well.

🚩 Inclusivity & Trigger Warnings

PPP is committed to creating an inclusive and supportive environment. A system of trigger warnings will be in place for sessions that include sensitive content, such as strong language, flashing imagery, or disturbing material.

You can learn more about our inclusivity commitments here:
👉 ppp.lossmeril.art/#inclusivity

📍 Venue Addresses

  • Ellison Building B 003 – Ellison Place, Newcastle upon Tyne NE1 8ST
  • Sandyford Building 405 – Sandyford Road, Newcastle upon Tyne NE1 8QE

Both buildings are located on the Northumbria University city campus. Please check signage or ask event staff if you need help navigating.

If you have any questions or accessibility needs, don’t hesitate to reach out.
